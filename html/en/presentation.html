Natural Escape

A world tour in 5 steps.

Welcome to Natural Escape: Explore the world and live the natural adventure.

Discover the world in a new way with Natural Escape, your gateway to a unique and memorable journey. Our mission is to offer you unforgettable experiences by taking you through extraordinary destinations, from wild Africa to ancient Asia, tropical rivers to majestic canyons. Our meticulously designed itineraries allow you to immerse yourself in the natural beauty of our planet, with carefully selected stops that will leave you with lifelong memories.



Our destinations :

1. Kruger Park, South Africa
Immerse yourself in the heart of African wildlife at Kruger National Park. Encounter the majestic Big Five on thrilling safaris and soak in the richness of the flora and fauna of this iconic national park.

2. Angkor City, Cambodge
Travel back in time at Angkor City, a dazzling archaeological site. Explore ancient temples, admire exquisite architecture, and uncover the fascinating history of this lost civilization.

3. Sepik River, Papua New Guinea
Sail the mystical waters of the Sepik River, discover unique tribal cultures, and delve into the fascinating art and traditions of this hidden gem in the Pacific.

4. Colca Canyon, Peru
Explore one of the world's deepest canyons, Colca Canyon in Peru. Take in breathtaking panoramic views, encounter majestic condors, and discover the rich culture of local communities.

5. Land of ice and fire, Island
Be mesmerized by Iceland's stunning landscapes, where glaciers meet volcanoes. Explore powerful geysers, majestic waterfalls, and natural hot springs, creating a natural symphony of ice and fire.

Why would you chose Natural Escape ?

At Natural Escape, we believe in authenticity, adventure, and preserving our planet. Our journey is crafted responsibly, respecting local communities and minimizing our impact on the environment. By choosing Natural Escape, you opt for a travel experience that goes beyond the beaten paths, allowing you to connect with nature and explore the world in a way that changes your perspective. Join us on this extraordinary adventure. Book your Natural Escape today and explore the world in a manner that nourishes your soul and broadens your horizons.
