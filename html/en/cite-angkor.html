Angkor City

The city of Angkor is a historical and architectural marvel nestled in the heart of lush nature, making it a must-visit destination for enthusiasts. Located in Cambodia, this site will impress you with its unique environment.

This iconic site, famous for its temple complex, including the Angkor Wat temple, one of the world's largest religious monuments, is surrounded by dense vegetation, creating a mystical and serene atmosphere. You will be amazed by how millennia-old tree roots have embraced the stones, creating a unique fusion of nature and history. The Angkor Archaeological Park is also dotted with numerous lakes and ponds, where the landscape is reflected in the water, creating picturesque scenes for photographers and nature observers.

Adventure enthusiasts can choose bike tours that take them to explore the surrounding countryside, with its local villages, terraced rice fields, and stunning natural landscapes. Angkor City is an exceptional destination that harmoniously combines ancient history with natural beauty. It invites you to discover the richness of Cambodian wildlife and flora while enjoying the majesty of ancient temples.

Activities

We offer you a two-day guided tour of the main Angkor site. Thus, you can enjoy a leisure day on the third day with no planned activities. You can revisit places you particularly enjoyed, discover new ones on your own, interact with other tourists, or explore the city's surroundings. You can especially take a bike excursion through local villages, along forest trails, where you'll encounter many species you've never seen before. You can also learn more about the locals' way of life, so different from ours.
