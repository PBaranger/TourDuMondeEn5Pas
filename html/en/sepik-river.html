Sepik River

Sepik River is an extraordinary adventure destination that will captivate anyone. This majestic river stretches over 1,100 kilometers through pristine landscapes, offering a unique experience in the heart of nature.

Dive into an unforgettable adventure along the Sepik River, one of the last preserved freshwater ecosystems on our planet. The region is a true sanctuary for biodiversity, home to a wide variety of rare animal and plant species. Navigating along the Sepik River will give you the opportunity to discover isolated traditional villages, engage in cultural exchanges, and admire local art and craftsmanship, including the region's famous wooden sculptures.

Canoe excursions on the Sepik will allow you to explore the most remote areas of the river, where nature is at its peak, offering breathtaking landscapes, spectacular sunsets, and unforgettable encounters with wildlife. Exploring the Sepik River will immerse you in the heart of wild nature and fascinating tribal culture. If you dream of exploring remote regions and immersing yourself in the natural beauty of our planet, Sepik River is a must-visit destination.

Activities

During the three days we offer you to spend by the river, you can dedicate one to canoeing. The journey takes place along the magnificent forest that surrounds the river and its dwellings. You can also spend a day exploring local cultures, discovering how they handle wood. This will allow you to forge strong bonds with the inhabitants of these villages.
